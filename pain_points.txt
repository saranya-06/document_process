Client's Name: The Library of Congress (Library)

Key Pain Points/Challenges:
- Need for the development and manufacture of an electronic braille reader
- Transition from paper-based braille materials to electronic distribution
- Requirement to provide devices to all braille readers enrolled in the program
- Compliance with applicable laws and obtaining necessary certifications
- Design review and approval process
- Manufacturing and production planning
- Maintenance and service program for braille readers
- Availability of spare parts for field replacement or repair
- Warranty and support for braille readers
- Intellectual property and data rights
